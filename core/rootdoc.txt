Welcome to the ''Graph for Scala'' API reference.
Some suggested navigation entry points:

  - '''graph editing''':
    - operators of [[scalax.collection.Graph `Graph`]]
    - or the operators of [[scalax.collection.mutable.Graph `mutable.Graph`]] and its inner nodes.

  - '''predefined edges''':
    - classes in the [[scalax.collection.GraphEdge$ `GraphEdge`]] object
    - classes in the [[scalax.collection.edge.package `edge`]] package
    - implicit members of the [[scalax.collection.GraphPredef$ `GraphPredef`]] object and
    - operators in the accompanying [[scalax.collection.edge.Implicits$ `Implicits`]] object.

  - '''graph traversal''':
    - methods of [[scalax.collection.GraphTraversal `GraphTraversal`]] and [[http://www.scala-graph.org/api/core/api/#scalax.collection.GraphTraversal$TraverserInnerNode `TraverserInnerNode`]].

  - '''random graphs'''
    [[scalax.collection.generator.RandomGraph `RandomGraph`]].

  - '''Scalacheck graph generators'''
    [[scalax.collection.generator.GraphGen `GraphGen`]].
